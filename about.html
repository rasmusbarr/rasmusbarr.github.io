About

This is Rasmus Barringer's personal website; new and sparsely populated as of 2016. Information here is presented in the hope that it will be useful and/or interesting. This includes tricks or algorithms I come up with and possibly some information about projects I'm working on or have finished. Algorithms presented are likely original (to the extent of my knowledge), unless otherwise is stated, and I put no restriction on their use by anyone (no warranty of course). Any supplied source code will have license information provided (likely the MIT license).

I did my PhD at Lund University in Sweden. For my university work, visit http://cs.lth.se/rasmus_barringer. Please do not contact me on the university email since I don't have access to it. Use Twitter (@rasmusbarr) or gmail (rasmus.barringer).
